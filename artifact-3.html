Delivery Type

A YouTube Uploaded Video

The class was asked to prepare the final video files, and after several rounds of feedback, we needed to upload them to YouTube.

Video Essay aimed to encourage
students revisit the course material
and examine technologies that led to major changes in the Hollywood.

Humans are often so occupied in moving forward that they almost forget to appreciate their history, and this is espcially true when it comes to the technologies we see in action daily. This assignment aimed to tackle just that by encouraging students to select one set of technology, revisit its past, and examine what changes in that technology led to changes in what we see now in the film industry.

Time to Reflect Back!

How did I do - My Thoughts




Argument

I would say the purpose of this assignment is to get more acquainted with the overall dynamics of working in a smaller (yet closer to a real world) team to achieve a common goal.

The way we were supposed to tackle the overall challenge seems to have made this visible. We divided the work among ourselves on the basis of our areas of expertise and later compiled everything together through several group meetings to produce our final product.




Genre / Media

The media we used for this artefact composed of portraits of various prominent people during the camera tech revolution, snippets from movies showcasing changes brought in by changes in technology, and images / illustrations of the technology we discuss in the artifact.

How we went ahead with this was through the compilation of all the research we did and the information we gather related to camera technologies over the course of this semester and summarizing all the information through a video essay.

Goals

I would consider the primary goal of the assignment to get the students work together as a group to work through the process of coming up with an idea, compiling everything that we had learnt throughout the course, meshing in the most important information that might be relevant to the chosen topic, and later merging all of them to produce the final product.

In terms of the communication strategies, this assignment aimed at all five of the communication skills from the WOVEN method â€“ written, oral, visual, electronic, and non-verbal.

Audience

Primarily, I would assume the intended audience of this assignment to be those who are interested in knowing more about the technologies used in the films and how did the films evolve in response to the technological advancements happening all over the globe.

Speaking specifically, the audience of this assignment could also be those looking to get more insights into how the technological advancements with camera brought changes in the film industry and what caused the near-downfall moments for the color photography technology.



Intended Revisions

If we had more time, we might have interviewed one of the experts in camera technology industry to provide their opinion on how the film industry responded to technological advancements.

Probably, we might have also prepared a better list of clips (the ones that conveyed our idea a bit more explicitly) to show in the artefact.
